Job Description


A software engineer develops information systems by designing, developing, and installing software solutions.


Salary Range


$82,000-$90,000 annually with a $85,000 median


Required Education or Skills


Becoming a software engineer requires at least a Bachelor's degree in software engineering. Good skills to have include being able to analyze information, having a good understanding of programming, being a good problem solver, and being a team player.


Why I want This Job


This job interests me because it is a job that involves the use of a computer and creating programs for different forms of technology.
